About Me

I am a second-year MPhil student at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou). I'm lucky to be advised by Prof. Mingming Fan and Prof. Yi Cai, affiliated with the APEX Lab. My research interest include Human-Computer Interaction, AR/VR, and Accessibility. I'm also passionate about exploring new opportunities in Human-AI collaboration.
Prior to joining HKUST(GZ), I obtained my bachelor’s degree in Industrial Design from Tongji University in 2023. My previous design and engineering projects include: interaction design, wearable technology, serious games, and AR/VR.
